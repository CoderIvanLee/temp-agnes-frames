# 《饼干碎了：It's Okay!》视频连续性规划

## 核心原则

**上一个分镜视频结束的状态 = 下一个分镜首帧图的状态**

每个分镜视频必须描述清楚：
1. 角色动作（从哪到哪）
2. 对话内容（谁说、说什么、什么表情）
3. 视频结束时角色的最终状态（姿势、表情、手中物品位置）

下一个分镜首帧图必须与上一个分镜视频结束状态完全一致。

---

## 分镜连续性设计

### 001 → 002 衔接

**001 视频结束状态**：
- Mommy 右手端着饼干盘，左手拿起一块饼干，伸向 Leo
- Leo 双手向前伸，准备接饼干，眼睛盯着饼干
- 饼干还在 Mommy 手中，未到 Leo 手里

**002 首帧图状态**（需与 001 结束一致）：
- Mommy 右手端着饼干盘，左手拿着饼干伸向 Leo
- Leo 双手已伸到饼干位置，手指即将碰到饼干
- 饼干在两人之间

**002 视频结束状态**：
- Leo 的手指碰到饼干，饼干开始从 Mommy 手中滑落
- Mommy 表情惊讶，眼睛瞪大
- Leo 眼睛也瞪大，嘴巴微张

**003 首帧图状态**（需与 002 结束一致）：
- 饼干已从 Leo 手中掉落，在空中或刚落地
- Mommy 表情惊讶
- Leo 眼睛瞪大，嘴巴微张，看着饼干掉落

## 逐镜视频提示词

### 001 - 妈妈递饼干给 Leo（4s）

**首帧图描述**：Mommy 端着饼干盘走向 Leo，Leo 站在高脚椅旁，双手搓着期待

**视频提示词**：
```
A bright modern kitchen dining corner with warm morning sunlight from a large window on the left. A mother stands on the left side of the frame, holding a ceramic plate of fresh golden cookies up toward a toddler boy. The toddler boy stands next to a wooden high chair on the right side, both hands rubbed together in excitement, big sparkling eyes looking at the cookies, mouth slightly open in anticipation.

Audio: Mother says cheerfully "Here you go, sweetie! Fresh cookies!" with a bright welcoming smile, leaning slightly forward.

Animate the mother with subtle arm movement as she extends the cookie plate forward and picks up one cookie with her left hand, offering it toward the boy. The boy stops rubbing his hands and extends both hands forward toward the cookie, fingers reaching out. His eyes follow the cookie as it moves closer. His body leans slightly forward. The mother's expression shifts from warm smile to cheerful delivery. Keep the face, outfit, kitchen scene, table, and lighting consistent. End state: mother's left hand holds one cookie extended toward the boy, the boy's hands are reaching toward the cookie, fingers close to it.
```
