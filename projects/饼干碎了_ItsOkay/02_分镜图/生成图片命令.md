## 生成方法

### 方法一：使用 run_image_gen_with_project_env.py（推荐）

适用于从文本生成全新图片。

```bash
cd /Users/amy/video_maker/projects/饼干碎了_ItsOkay

python3 /Users/amy/my_project/Agents/douyin/tools/run_image_gen_with_project_env.py \
  image_gen.py \
  --prompt "Medium two-shot, mother and toddler boy at wooden dining table in warm morning kitchen. Mother on left, wearing white off-shoulder top and yellow flower skirt, long dark curly hair. Toddler boy on right in high chair, wearing blue dinosaur t-shirt and white pants, black short hair. Mother holding ceramic plate of golden cookies, smiling warmly. Boy reaching hand toward cookies with excited expression. Warm morning sunlight, soft shadows, Pixar style, Disney style, 3D clay render, C4D, Blender 3D, ultra-detailed textures, subsurface scattering, 8k, masterpiece" \
  --width 1280 \
  --height 720 \
  --image /Users/amy/video_maker/shared/scenes/kitchen_dining_v1.png \
  --image /Users/amy/video_maker/shared/characters/mommy/Mommy-China.png \
  --image /Users/amy/video_maker/shared/characters/leo/baby.png \
  --seed 42 \
  --steps 30 \
  --guidance_scale 7.5
```

**关键规则**：
1. **必须传入所有参考图**：`--image` 参数至少包含场景图 + 每个角色的一张参考图
2. **prompt 中描述角色外貌**：服装、发型、颜色等视觉特征必须写在 prompt 中
3. **输出路径**：保存到 `02_分镜图/关键帧/` 目录
4. **文件名格式**：`{序号}_{描述}_首帧_v{n}.png`

### 方法二：使用 dreamina image2image

适用于基于参考图进行风格转换或动作调整。

```bash
dreamina image2image \
  --images "/Users/amy/video_maker/projects/饼干碎了_ItsOkay/02_分镜图/关键帧/001_Mommy_Leo_递饼干_首帧_v1.png,/Users/amy/video_maker/projects/饼干碎了_ItsOkay/03_视频片段/001_Mommy_Leo_递饼干_视频_v6.mp4_extracted_last_frame.png,/Users/amy/video_maker/shared/scenes/kitchen_dining_v1.png,/Users/amy/video_maker/shared/characters/mommy/Mommy-China.png,/Users/amy/video_maker/shared/characters/leo/baby.png" \
  --prompt "Medium two-shot, mother and toddler boy at wooden dining table. Mother on left, sitting upright, right hand holding ceramic plate of golden cookies steady on table edge, left hand holding one cookie extended forward toward boy. Boy in high chair on right, right hand reaching up toward cookie, mouth slightly open in anticipation. Both have warm expectant smiles. Same appearances as reference images, Pixar style, Disney style, 3D character render, C4D render, Blender 3D, ultra-detailed textures, soft cinematic lighting, warm cozy atmosphere, subsurface scattering, 8k resolution, masterpiece, clay style, 16:9 horizontal composition" \
  --resolution_type 2k \
  --poll 30
```

**关键规则**：
1. **图1（固定）**：`001_Mommy_Leo_递饼干_首帧_v1.png` — 作为角色外貌和服装的基准参考
2. **图2（动态）**：前一个分镜视频的最后一帧 — 作为动作和表情的参考
3. **后续图片**：场景参考图 + 角色参考图（按顺序排列）
4. **prompt 描述**：让图1中的人物动作表情与图2保持一致
5. **输出路径**：保存到 `02_分镜图/关键帧/` 目录

1. **禁止删除/覆盖已验证的关键帧图**
